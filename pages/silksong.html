Silksong

Silksong is the second game made by Team Cherry. It took them 7 YEARS to make, and everyone went crazy when they announced the release date, which was on september 4.

Here are some facts about the game:

  • You play as Hornet from the first game Hollow Knight
  • The game definitly shares the same DNA as the original, but it's more polished and improved.
  • It is a lot harder because of the nature of Hornets mouvement and the setting in general is more fast paced.
